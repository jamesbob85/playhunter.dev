Ghost Tac Toe

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 31, 2026

Ghost Tac Toe is a free mobile game developed by PlayHunter for Android. This page explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

In short: Ghost Tac Toe collects no data in offline mode. If you choose to play online ranked matches, we collect only what is necessary to run multiplayer — your player name, match results, and rating. We do not use ads, analytics, or tracking.

1. Offline Play (No Data Collection)

When playing offline (vs AI or local 2-player), Ghost Tac Toe does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The game works entirely on your device with no network calls.

2. Online Ranked Play

If you choose to use the optional online ranked mode, the following data is collected and processed to enable multiplayer functionality:

Data We Collect

Data Purpose Stored Where
Google Play Games player ID Identify you in matches Server (Supabase)
Display name Show your name to opponents Server (Supabase)
Match history Record game outcomes and moves Server (Supabase)
Rating & rank Matchmaking and leaderboards Server (Supabase)
Win/loss/draw stats Display on your profile Server (Supabase)

This data is transmitted securely via HTTPS to our database hosted on Supabase (supabase.com). We do not sell, share, or use this data for any purpose other than running the game.

Real-Time Communication

During online matches, the app maintains a real-time connection (WebSocket) to synchronize moves between players. This connection is active only during gameplay and transmits only game state data (board positions, turns, and match status).

3. Google Play Games Services

Ghost Tac Toe uses Google Play Games Services for:

  • Sign-in: Authenticate your identity for online play using your existing Google account. We do not create a separate account.
  • Achievements: Track and display in-game achievements. Achievement data is managed by Google.

Google Play Games Services is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We receive only your player ID and display name from Google — we do not access your email, contacts, or other Google account data.

4. Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Game statistics for offline modes (wins, losses, draws)
  • User preferences (sound, haptics, language)
  • Achievement progress
  • Cached player profile for faster loading

This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

5. Analytics

Ghost Tac Toe uses Firebase Analytics (provided by Google) to understand how players use the app and improve the experience. This is enabled for all users.

What Analytics Collects

  • App usage events (games started, games completed, modes played, settings changed)
  • Session data (how often and how long you use the app)
  • Device information (device model, OS version, screen size)
  • App performance data (launch time, errors)

Analytics data is anonymous and aggregated — it is not linked to your identity. We use this data only to understand gameplay patterns and improve the app. Firebase Analytics is governed by Firebase's Privacy Policy.

6. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No advertising IDs or ad tracking
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, or device files
  • No email addresses
  • No financial or payment information (the app has no in-app purchases)

7. Children's Privacy

Ghost Tac Toe does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The app contains no user-generated content, no chat features, no social features, and no in-app purchases. Online play requires a Google Play Games account, which is subject to Google's age requirements.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

Online player data (profile, match history, ratings) is retained as long as you use the service. To request deletion of your server-side data, contact us at the address below. Local data can be deleted at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to request deletion of your data, contact us at playhunter.dev or through the Google Play Store listing.
