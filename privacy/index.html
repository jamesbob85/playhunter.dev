Ghost Tac Toe

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 29, 2026

Ghost Tac Toe is a free mobile game developed by PlayHunter for Android. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use the app.

Data Collection

Ghost Tac Toe does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, phone, location, etc.)
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No advertising SDKs or ad networks are integrated
  • No data is sent to any server — the app works entirely offline
  • No account creation or sign-in is required

Local Storage

The app stores game statistics (wins, losses, draws) and user preferences (sound and haptics settings) locally on your device using standard device storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be deleted by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app

Children's Privacy

Ghost Tac Toe does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children under 13. The app contains no user-generated content, no chat features, no social features, and no in-app purchases.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services that may collect information. No ads, analytics, crash reporting, or social media SDKs are included.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at playhunter.dev or through the Google Play Store listing.
